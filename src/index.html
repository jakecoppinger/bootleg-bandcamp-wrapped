Bootleg Bandcamp Wrapped 2025

A fork of Tom MacWright's Bandcamp (unofficially) wrapped

Intro

It's like Spotify wrapped but for Bandcamp instead! Share the albums you bought this year and see how much you paid artists!

It should go without saying this is not affiliated with Bandcamp in any way. I like to buy music on Bandcamp because on average artists get paid 82% of the fee (including payment processor fees), rather than fractions of a cent per stream.

This demo is a fork of Tom MacWright's unofficial Bandcamp wrapped with some extra features. You may like to try his version at https://tmcw-bandcampwrapped.web.val.run/ or read about his project and (his music wrapped) on his blog!

How to

Go to Bandcamp, click on your icon in the top right, and click on the purchases item. Scroll down and click 'View all ––– purchases' if you need to. Scroll to the first purchase this year, and select all of the text from that purchase to the top of the list, and hit copy. Then paste into the textarea above. This might be a little tricky, but just focus on, y'know, copying the right content. Mouse down in the gap after the first album of 2025, drag up till you're in the gap before the last album of 2025 and all the text in those albums is highlighted.
